### 3. Apple Reseller Requirements

#### Options:

**A. Apple Authorized Reseller (AAR)**
- Requires: Business entity, retail location or approved online presence
- Benefits: Direct Apple pricing (typically 5-8% margin), co-op marketing funds
- Challenges: Minimum order requirements, strict pricing guidelines, lengthy approval

**B. Wholesale/Distribution Partners**
- Partners: Ingram Micro, Tech Data, D&H, Synnex
- Benefits: No Apple approval needed, flexible ordering
- Margins: 3-6% typical

**C. Apple Refurbished Program**
- Source: Apple Certified Refurbished directly from Apple
- Benefits: Lower cost, same warranty, environmentally friendly
- Margins: Can achieve 15-20% by buying at retail refurb prices

**Recommended Approach: Hybrid**
1. Start with wholesale distribution (Ingram Micro/Tech Data)
2. Apply for Apple Authorized Reseller status
3. Offer refurbished options as budget tier
4. Position as VAR (Value-Added Reseller) with OpenClaw pre-install

### 4. Pricing & Margin Strategy

#### Revenue Streams
1. **Hardware Markup:** 8-18% on Mac mini base price
2. **OpenClaw License:** Recurring annual subscription ($99-$199/yr)
3. **Setup Fee:** $49 one-time for pre-configuration (built into price)
4. **Support Packages:** Optional premium support ($29/mo)
5. **Accessories:** Keyboards, mice, monitors (optional add-ons)

#### Competitive Analysis
- Bare Mac mini: $599-$1,999 (Apple direct)
- Our value-add: $150-$300 premium includes:
  - OpenClaw license (first year)
  - Pre-configuration and testing
  - Setup guide and onboarding
  - 30-day priority support

### 5. Fulfillment & Logistics

#### Order Flow
1. Customer places order on website
2. Payment processed via Stripe
3. Order notification sent to fulfillment
4. Source Mac mini (wholesale/refurb/direct)
5. OpenClaw installation and configuration (1-2 hours per unit)
6. Quality assurance testing
7. Package with branded materials
8. Ship via UPS/FedEx (insured)
9. Send tracking + onboarding email

#### Fulfillment Options
- **Phase 1:** Manual fulfillment from home office
- **Phase 2:** Partner with 3PL for storage/shipping
- **Phase 3:** Scale with dedicated fulfillment center

#### Shipping
- Continental US: Free ground shipping (3-5 days)
- Expedited: $29 (2-day)
- Overnight: $49
- International: Quote on request

### 6. Supplier Strategy

#### Primary Sources
1. **Ingram Micro** - Major Apple distributor, net-30 terms available
2. **Tech Data (TD Synnex)** - Alternative distributor
3. **Apple Refurbished Store** - For budget options

#### Inventory Strategy
- **Build-to-Order:** Initial phase, no inventory risk
- **Stock Popular Models:** Once demand is proven (M4 16GB, M4 Pro 24GB)
- **Target Inventory:** 5-10 units of top 2 configs after validation
