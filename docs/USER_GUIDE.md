## First Boot Setup

### Step 1: Connect Your Hardware

1. Place the Mac mini near your display
2. Connect the power cable to the Mac mini
3. Connect your display via HDMI or USB-C
4. Connect keyboard and mouse (USB ports on back)
5. Optionally connect Ethernet cable

### Step 2: Power On

1. Press the power button (bottom-right on the back)
2. You'll see the Apple logo, then the login screen
3. Use the temporary credentials on your Welcome card

### Step 3: Connect to Internet

1. Click the WiFi icon in the menu bar
2. Select your network
3. Enter your password
4. Wait for connection

### Step 4: Activate Your License

1. OpenClaw should start automatically
2. When prompted, enter your license key (from the License Card)
3. Click "Activate"
4. You're ready to go!

## Using OpenClaw

### Launching OpenClaw

OpenClaw starts automatically when you log in. Look for the icon in your menu bar (top-right of screen).

**If it's not running:**
- Open Finder
- Go to Applications
- Double-click OpenClaw

### Your First Conversation

1. Click the OpenClaw icon in the menu bar
2. A chat window will appear
3. Type your question or request
4. Press Enter

**Example prompts to try:**
- "What can you help me with?"
- "Summarize the latest news about AI"
- "Write a short poem about coffee"

### Getting Better Results

1. **Be specific**: Instead of "write an email," try "write a professional email to my manager asking for time off next Friday"

2. **Provide context**: "I'm working on a Python project that processes CSV files. How do I..."

3. **Ask follow-ups**: If the first response isn't quite right, ask OpenClaw to revise or clarify

4. **Use examples**: "Write something similar to this: [paste example]"

### Privacy Best Practices

- All processing happens locally on your Mac
- Your conversations are stored only on your device
- No data is sent to cloud servers
- For extra security, enable FileVault disk encryption

## Troubleshooting

### OpenClaw Won't Start

1. Restart your Mac (Apple menu ‚Üí Restart)
2. If still not working, check Activity Monitor:
   - Open Applications ‚Üí Utilities ‚Üí Activity Monitor
   - Search for "OpenClaw"
   - If found, select it and click the X to quit
   - Try launching OpenClaw again

### License Issues

**"Invalid License" Error:**
1. Verify your license key is entered correctly
2. Check your internet connection
3. Contact support if problem persists

**"License Expired" Warning:**
1. Visit openclawmac.com/renew
2. Log in with your email
3. Complete renewal process

### Slow Performance

1. Close unused applications
2. Check available memory (Apple menu ‚Üí About This Mac ‚Üí Memory)
3. Restart your Mac
4. If using heavy models, consider upgrading to Pro/Enterprise tier

### No Internet Required (After Setup)

Once activated, OpenClaw works completely offline. The AI runs locally on your Mac's Apple Silicon chip.
